About Good On Paper Improv

Good On Paper is a six-person improv troupe in the Traverse City area. Assembled in 2010, Good On Paper sought to bring "Big City" improvised comedy to Traverse City, inspired by the likes of Second City, iO, and The Groundlings. This high energy group creates hilarious on-the-spot comedy using audience suggestions and volunteers.

No two shows are the same. Come put your suggestions in the Can-o-Words, grab a drink, and enjoy 90 minutes of gut-busting, totally improvised humor! Shows are on the second Saturday of every month at West Bay Beach Resort, 615 E Front Street. Tickets are $10 at the door. Doors open at 7:30pm, show starts at 8pm.

"The Traverse City area's only professional improv comedy troupe, creating comedy scenes based entirely from audience suggestions. Highly energetic, completely interactive."
